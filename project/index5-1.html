Gulab jamun


Ingrediants:


  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipy:


  1. Mix milk powder, maida, and baking powder to form a soft dough.
  2. Shape the dough into small balls and deep fry until golden brown.
  3. Soak the fried balls in warm sugar syrup for 1-2 hours before serving.

Khoa barfi


Ingrediants:


  • Khoa
  • Bread
  • Milk

Recipy


  1. Mix milk powder, sugar, and ghee in a pan over low heat.
  2. Stir continuously until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  3. Pour into a greased tray, flatten, let it cool, and cut into squares.

Orange Juice


Ingrediants:


  • 4 fresh oranges
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Recipy:


  1. Cut and squeeze the oranges to extract juice.
  2. Strain the juice to remove seeds and pulp.
  3. Serve chilled with ice and sugar if desired.

Chole Bhature


Ingrediants:


  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 2 bhature (deep-fried bread)
  • 2 tablespoons chole masala

Recipy:


  1. Cook chickpeas with onions, tomatoes, and chole masala.
  2. Prepare dough and deep-fry bhature until golden.
  3. Serve hot chole with fluffy bhature and onion rings.

Naan Chole


Ingrediants:


  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas (chole)
  • 2 pieces of fresh naan
  • 1 tablespoon chole masala

Recipy:


  1. Saute onions, tomatoes, and spices in a pan, then add boiled chickpeas and chole masala. Cook for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Warm the naan on a tawa or in the oven until soft and slightly crisp.
  3. Serve hot chole with naan, garnished with chopped onions and coriander.
